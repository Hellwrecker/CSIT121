Your New Favorite Tomato Soup Recipe

Created by George Biniaris

Ingredients:

  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Peel and chop the onions and garlic.
  2. Peel the tomatoes and remove the seeds.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  4. Add the onions and garlic to the saucepan and cook until they are soft and translucent.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes to the saucepan and let cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the sugar, salt, and black pepper to the saucepan and stir well.
  7. Using an immersion blender, puree the soup until it is smooth.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
